Krabby Patty - Spongebob Squarepants

Description

Deep under the sea at the Krusty Krab, one burger reigns supreme: the legendary Krabby Patty. While Mr. Krabs keeps the secret formula under lock and key, we suspect it might involve a beachy classic like the Chesapeake region’s Old Bay seasoning. Potatorolls.com's Old Bay Burger, served on Martin’s Big Marty’s Rolls and stacked with all the fixings, is a pretty solid guess—and a delicious one at that. And if that doesn’t quite satisfy your seafood craving, try our Crab Cake Sliders for a tasty twist. Just… don’t tell Mr. Krabs. This recipe serves 6 people.

Ingredients

  • 11/2 Pounds Ground Beef
  • 1 Small Onion, diced
  • 1 Slice Martin's Potato Bread, torn into tiny pieces
  • 1 Egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 Tablespoons Old Bay Seasoning
  • 3 Tablespoons Fresh Parsley, chopped
  • 1 Teaspoon Mustard
  • 1 Teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce
  • 6 Martin's Big Marty's rolls, for serving
  • Tomato Slices, for serving
  • Cheese, for serving
  • Lettuce, for serving

Steps

  1. Combine all of the patty ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Form into 6 evenly sized patties.
  3. Heat a grill to high heat. Place the burgers on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
    Serve on Martin's Big Marty Rolls, with desired toppings.
