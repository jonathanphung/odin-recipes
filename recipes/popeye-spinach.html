Salami and Spinach Scramble on Toast - Popeye

Description

Popeye didn’t need much to save the day—just a can of spinach and a little muscle. This classic cartoon moment turned leafy greens into a symbol of strength, and now you can enjoy them in a far tastier form. Potatorolls.com's Salami and Spinach Scramble on Toast combines tender sautéed spinach and onions, chopped salami, and softly scrambled eggs, all served alongside toasted Martin’s Potato Bread. It’s a hearty, power-packed breakfast that’ll have you feeling strong to the finish. This recipe serves between 4-6 people.

Ingredients

  • 8-12 Slices Martin's Potato Bread, toasted
  • 1/8 Cup Canola Oil
  • 1 Large Onion, thinly sliced
  • 10 Ounces Genoa Salami, cut into 1/4- inch-thick strips
  • 4 Cups Baby Arugula or Spinach
  • 1 Dozen Large Eggs, lightly beaten
  • Salt and Freshly Ground Pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat canola oil in a large nonstick skillet
  2. Add sliced onion to pan and cook over moderately high heat, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add salami strips to pan and cook until lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add arugula (or spinach) and cook until just wilted, about 1 minute.
  5. Add eggs and cook, stirring gently, until eggs are softly set, about 2 minutes.
  6. Transfer the mixture to plates and serve with toasted Martin's Potato Bread.
